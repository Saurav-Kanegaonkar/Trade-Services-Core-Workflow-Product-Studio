# Data Dictionary

## `workflow_opportunities.csv`

- `opportunity_id`: unique workflow opportunity identifier.
- `product_area`: core platform area, such as scheduling, estimates, invoicing, or payments.
- `trade_vertical`: trade service vertical used for scenario context.
- `company_size`: synthetic customer size segment.
- `persona`: primary user or buyer persona affected by the workflow.
- `workflow_stage`: lifecycle stage where friction occurs.
- `customer_pain`: PM-ready pain statement.
- `hypothesis`: feature or workflow bet to validate.
- `frequency_pct`: modeled frequency of the pain signal.
- `severity`: modeled user pain severity.
- `adoption_impact`: expected effect on product adoption.
- `cash_flow_impact`: expected effect on customer revenue collection or business flow.
- `effort`: relative implementation effort.
- `dependency_risk`: relative cross-system or cross-team risk.
- `confidence`: confidence in the evidence pattern.
- `enablement_need`: main Sales, Support, or Customer Success need.
- `priority_score`: weighted score generated by the analysis script.
- `decision`: recommended action.

## `customer_research_themes.csv`

Customer pain themes, source mix, quote patterns, observed friction, evidence count, and proposed product response.

## `prd_requirements.csv`

Requirements, user stories, acceptance criteria, API or system concepts, telemetry events, priority, and status for the top product opportunity.

## `launch_gates.csv`

Launch gate name, owner, required evidence, status, risk, and next step.

## `enablement_assets.csv`

Enablement asset, audience, purpose, owner, due stage, and readiness score.
